PROJECT-

I've made up an character of an invented popular voice actor and created it's curriculum. 
Since it's a more creative work, i've prefered to make an non so formal curriculum 
giving it a more colorful and dinamic aspect. The target profile df the curriculum are particular enterprises related to entretainment sector.

For the visual desing I have chosen to inspire me in the desing of one of my favourites videogames character, Sett from the game League of Legends. I chose the palette and put decorations related to him.
